## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Sales Pipeline View Dashboard module provides comprehensive visualization and analytics for the entire sales pipeline, offering real-time insights into deal progression, revenue forecasting, and sales performance metrics. It serves as the central command center for sales teams, managers, and executives to monitor pipeline health, identify bottlenecks, track conversion rates, and make data-driven decisions for revenue optimization and sales strategy adjustments.

### **2.2 Business Scope**

**In Scope:**
- Real-time sales pipeline visualization with interactive kanban-style board and list views
- Advanced filtering and segmentation capabilities by stage, owner, region, and time periods
- Revenue forecasting with probability-based calculations and confidence intervals
- Sales performance analytics with conversion rates, deal velocity, and win/loss metrics
- Pipeline health monitoring with aging analysis, risk indicators, and trend analysis
- Executive reporting with comparative metrics and strategic insights
- Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go pipeline monitoring and management
- Customizable widgets and layouts for role-based dashboard configurations
- Real-time alerts and notifications for critical pipeline events and milestones

**Out of Scope:**
- Opportunity creation and management (handled by Opportunity Pipeline module)
- Quote generation and pricing management (handled by Quote Creation module)
- Account and contact data management (handled by Account Management modules)
- Advanced financial calculations and billing (handled by external ERP systems)
- Customer service and support case management (handled by Customer Service modules)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Sales Representatives:** Monitor personal pipeline performance, track deal progression, and identify coaching opportunities
- **Sales Managers:** Analyze team pipeline health, monitor performance metrics, and provide strategic guidance
- **Sales Directors:** Review regional pipeline performance, revenue forecasts, and strategic sales planning
- **Sales Operations:** Monitor system performance, data quality, and dashboard utilization metrics
- **Finance Teams:** Review revenue forecasts, deal economics, and financial impact analysis
- **Executives:** Access high-level dashboards for strategic decision-making and business planning

class SalesPipelineDashboard(models.Model):

    dashboard_name = models.CharField(max_length=100)

    is_default = models.BooleanField(default=False)
    is_public = models.BooleanField(default=False)
    refresh_interval = models.IntegerField(default=300)  # 5 minutes
    
    # Metadata
    created_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now_add=True)
    updated_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now=True)
    last_accessed = models.DateTimeField(null=True, blank=True)

        db_table = 'crm_sales_pipeline_dashboards'

## **4. UI/UX SPECIFICATIONS**

### **4.1 User Interface Requirements**

- **Layout:** Responsive grid-based dashboard layout with drag-and-drop widget positioning and customizable layouts
- **Navigation:** Intuitive navigation with tab-based views for different dashboard types and quick access to key metrics
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile-optimized interface with touch-optimized interactions and adaptive widget sizing

- **User Flow:** Login → Dashboard Selection → View Pipeline Metrics → Apply Filters → Analyze Data → Export Reports

- **WCAG Compliance:** WCAG 2.1 AA level compliance with keyboard navigation for all dashboard interactions and data exploration

- **Color Scheme:** Professional color palette with semantic color coding for different pipeline statuses and performance indicators
- **Typography:** Clear, readable fonts with appropriate sizing for data visualization and dashboard text
