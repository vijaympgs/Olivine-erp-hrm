## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Knowledge Base module serves as the comprehensive master system for creating, organizing, and managing knowledge resources that support customer service operations. It provides organizations with powerful tools for building and maintaining a centralized repository of articles, FAQs, tutorials, and documentation that enable self-service support, reduce ticket volume, and improve customer satisfaction. The module facilitates knowledge creation, version control, categorization, search optimization, and analytics to ensure that support teams and customers have access to accurate, up-to-date information when they need it most.

### **2.2 Business Scope**

**In Scope:**
- Comprehensive article creation with rich text editing, media embedding, and version control
- Hierarchical category and tag management for logical knowledge organization
- Advanced search functionality with full-text search, filters, and intelligent recommendations
- Multi-language support with translation management and localization capabilities
- Content approval workflows with review processes and publication scheduling
- Analytics and reporting on article usage, search patterns, and knowledge gaps
- Integration with ticket systems for automatic article suggestions and knowledge linking
- User feedback collection with rating systems and improvement suggestions
- Content lifecycle management with archiving, expiration, and review scheduling
- Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go knowledge access and contribution

**Out of Scope:**
- Real-time customer support interactions (handled by Ticket System and Case Management modules)
- Advanced case management and legal documentation (handled by Case Management module)
- Customer feedback collection and analysis (handled by Customer Feedback module)
- Financial billing and subscription management (handled by external systems)
- Advanced user management and authentication (handled by core system modules)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Knowledge Managers:** Primary users responsible for knowledge strategy, content creation, and quality control
- **Support Agents:** Contribute articles, use knowledge for ticket resolution, and provide feedback
- **Technical Writers:** Create and maintain technical documentation and tutorials
- **Product Managers:** Provide product information and review technical accuracy
- **Customers:** Access knowledge base for self-service support and information
- **Executives:** Monitor knowledge base effectiveness and ROI on support operations

## **5. FOREIGN KEY RELATIONSHIPS**

### **5.1 Primary Relationships**

- **Article** → **Company (company_id):** Many-to-One relationship linking articles to companies for multi-tenant isolation
- **Article** → **Category (category_id):** Many-to-One relationship linking articles to their primary category
- **Article** → **User (created_by_user_id):** Many-to-One relationship tracking which user created the article
- **Article** → **User (updated_by_user_id):** Many-to-One relationship tracking the last user who modified the article
- **Article** → **User (reviewed_by_user_id):** Many-to-One relationship tracking the user who reviewed the article

- **ArticleVersion** → **Article (article_id):** Many-to-One relationship linking versions to their parent article
- **ArticleVersion** → **User (created_by_user_id):** Many-to-One relationship linking versions to the user who created them

### **5.2 Many-to-Many Relationships**

- **Article** ↔ **Tag:** Many-to-Many relationship through article_tags associations for content classification
- **Article** ↔ **User:** Many-to-Many relationship through article_favorites for user bookmarks
- **Article** ↔ **User:** Many-to-to-Many relationship through article_collaborators for collaborative editing

### **5.3 Relationship Integrity Rules**

- **Cascade Delete:** When an article is deleted, all associated versions are soft-deleted but history is preserved
- **Protect Delete:** Company and category records referenced in articles are protected from deletion
- **Restrict Delete:** Articles referenced in active ticket resolutions are restricted from deletion
- **Version Integrity:** Versions cannot exist without a parent article

### **5.4 Article Configuration Constraints**

- **Slug Uniqueness:** Article slugs must be unique within each company to prevent conflicts
- **Category Validation:** Articles must belong to an active category for publication
- **Status Transitions:** Articles must follow valid status transitions with proper approval workflows
- **Version Control:** Article versions must maintain sequential numbering and proper change tracking

```python
def clean_slug(self):
    """
    Validate article slug format and uniqueness
    """
    if not self.slug:
        raise ValidationError("Article slug is required")
    
    # Check uniqueness within company
    if Article.objects.filter(
        company_id=self.company_id,
        slug=self.slug,
        is_deleted=False
    ).exclude(id=self.id).exists():
        raise ValidationError("Article slug must be unique within the company")

def clean_status_transition(self):
    """
    Validate article status transitions
    """
    if self.status and self.pk:
        old_article = Article.objects.get(pk=self.pk)
        valid_transitions = {
            'draft': ['in_review', 'published', 'archived'],
            'in_review': ['draft', 'published', 'rejected'],
            'published': ['draft', 'archived', 'expired'],
            'archived': ['draft', 'published'],
            'rejected': ['draft', 'in_review'],
            'expired': ['draft', 'published']
        }
        
        if old_article.status != self.status:
            if self.status not in valid_transitions.get(old_article.status, []):
                raise ValidationError(f"Invalid status transition from {old_article.status} to {self.status}")

def clean_content_quality(self):
    """
    Validate article content quality and requirements
    """
    if self.content:
        word_count = len(self.content.split())
        if word_count < 100:
            raise ValidationError("Article content must be at least 100 words")
        
        # Check for basic HTML structure if content contains HTML
        if '<' in self.content and '>' in self.content:
            # Basic HTML validation
            if not self.content.strip().startswith('<'):
                raise ValidationError("HTML content must be properly formatted")

def clean_seo_requirements(self):
    """
    Validate SEO requirements for published articles
    """
    if self.status == 'published':
        if not self.meta_title or len(self.meta_title.strip()) < 10:
            raise ValidationError("Published articles must have a meta title of at least 10 characters")
        
        if not self.meta_description or len(self.meta_description.strip()) < 50:
            raise ValidationError("Published articles must have a meta description of at least 50 characters")
```

## **9. API SPECIFICATIONS**

### **9.1 REST Endpoints**

#### **GET /api/knowledge/articles/**
- **Purpose:** Retrieve paginated list of articles with filtering and search capabilities
- **Authentication:** JWT token required with company_id validation
- **Parameters:** page, page_size, status, category_id, tag, featured, date_range, search_query
- **Response:** Paginated article list with total count and filtering metadata

#### **POST /api/knowledge/articles/**
- **Purpose:** Create new article with validation and automatic slug generation
- **Authentication:** JWT token with create_article permission
- **Request Body:** Article data with required fields validation and content processing
- **Response:** Created article object with validation warnings and publication status

#### **GET /api/knowledge/articles/{id}/**
- **Purpose:** Retrieve detailed article information including content, versions, and analytics
- **Authentication:** JWT token with read_article permission
- **Parameters:** article_id (UUID), include_versions, include_analytics, include_related
- **Response:** Complete article object with related data and associations

#### **PUT /api/knowledge/articles/{id}/**
- **Purpose:** Update existing article information with version control and validation
- **Authentication:** JWT token with update_article permission
- **Request Body:** Updated article fields with validation and version creation
- **Response:** Updated article object with version information and change summary

#### **POST /api/knowledge/articles/{id}/publish/**
- **Purpose:** Publish article with validation and SEO optimization
- **Authentication:** JWT token with publish_article permission
- **Request Body:** Publication configuration with scheduling options and metadata
- **Response:** Publication confirmation with SEO analysis and search indexing status

#### **GET /api/knowledge/categories/**
- **Purpose:** Retrieve hierarchical category structure with article counts
- **Authentication:** JWT token with read_reference_data permission
- **Parameters:** parent_category_id, is_active, include_article_count
- **Response:** Category tree structure with filtering metadata

#### **GET /api/knowledge/tags/**
- **Purpose:** Retrieve list of tags with usage statistics and filtering capabilities
- **Authentication:** JWT token with read_reference_data permission
- **Parameters:** is_active, min_usage_count, search_query
- **Response:** Tag list with usage statistics and filtering metadata

#### **GET /api/knowledge/search/**
- **Purpose:** Perform full-text search across articles with intelligent ranking
- **Authentication:** JWT token with search_permission
- **Parameters:** query, category_id, tag_id, date_range, sort_by
- **Response:** Search results with relevance scoring and filtering options

### **9.2 Data Formats**

- **Request Format:** JSON for all endpoints except file uploads (multipart/form-data)
- **Response Format:** JSON with consistent error structure and pagination metadata
- **Error Format:** Standardized error response with error_code, message, and details fields
- **Date Format:** ISO 8601 (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:mm:ssZ) for all datetime fields
