## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Customer Feedback module serves as a comprehensive reporting system for collecting, analyzing, and reporting on customer feedback across all support interactions. It provides organizations with powerful tools for gathering customer satisfaction data, analyzing feedback trends, identifying improvement opportunities, and measuring support quality. The module enables systematic feedback collection through multiple channels, automated sentiment analysis, trend identification, and actionable insights to drive continuous improvement in customer service operations and enhance overall customer experience.

### **2.2 Business Scope**

**In Scope:**
- Multi-channel feedback collection from tickets, cases, surveys, and direct customer submissions
- Automated sentiment analysis with natural language processing and emotion detection
- Comprehensive feedback analytics with trend analysis, correlation studies, and predictive insights
- Customer satisfaction scoring with NPS, CSAT, and CES metrics calculation and tracking
- Feedback categorization and tagging for systematic analysis and reporting
- Real-time dashboards with interactive visualizations and drill-down capabilities
- Automated alert system for negative feedback and urgent customer issues
- Integration with ticket and case systems for contextual feedback analysis
- Custom report generation with scheduling and distribution capabilities
- Performance benchmarking against industry standards and historical data

**Out of Scope:**
- Real-time customer support interactions (handled by Ticket System and Case Management modules)
- Advanced knowledge base creation and management (handled by Knowledge Base module)
- Financial billing and invoicing systems (handled by external ERP systems)
- Advanced user management and authentication (handled by core system modules)
- Social media monitoring and engagement (handled by external social media platforms)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Customer Service Managers:** Primary users for feedback analysis, performance monitoring, and improvement planning
- **Support Agents:** Receive feedback on their performance and access customer insights for service improvement
- **Quality Assurance Teams:** Monitor feedback quality, analyze trends, and identify training needs
- **Product Managers:** Analyze product-related feedback for feature development and improvement
- **Executives:** Review overall customer satisfaction metrics and strategic insights
- **Customers:** Provide feedback through various channels and access their feedback history

## **4. REPORTING & ANALYTICS**

### **4.1 Key Performance Indicators**

- **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT):** Average customer satisfaction rating from feedback surveys
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Customer loyalty and recommendation likelihood score
- **Customer Effort Score (CES):** Ease of customer service interaction score
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Positive, negative, and neutral sentiment distribution percentages
- **Response Rate:** Percentage of customers who provide feedback when requested
- **First Contact Resolution Satisfaction:** Satisfaction scores for issues resolved on first contact
- **Agent Performance Ratings:** Average customer ratings for individual support agents
- **Feedback Volume:** Total number of feedback submissions per time period
- **Issue Resolution Time:** Correlation between resolution time and customer satisfaction
- **Category Analysis:** Satisfaction scores by feedback category and issue type

### **4.2 Dashboard Components**

#### **Executive Dashboard**
- Overall customer satisfaction trends with historical comparisons
- NPS trends with promoter, passive, and detractor breakdowns
- Feedback volume analysis by channel and time period
- Top performing agents and teams by customer ratings
- Critical issues requiring immediate attention
- Customer satisfaction benchmarking against industry standards

#### **Manager Dashboard**
- Team performance metrics with individual agent ratings
- Feedback trends by category and subcategory analysis
- Sentiment analysis with emotion detection and key topics
- Follow-up action tracking and resolution status
- Customer feedback correlation with ticket resolution times
- Training needs identification based on feedback patterns

#### **Agent Dashboard**
- Personal performance ratings and customer feedback summary
- Individual feedback history with detailed comments
- Skill gap analysis and improvement recommendations
- Customer recognition and positive feedback highlights
- Performance comparison with team averages
- Goal setting and progress tracking

### **4.3 Advanced Analytics**

- **Predictive Analytics:** Machine learning models to predict customer satisfaction based on interaction patterns
- **Trend Analysis:** Statistical analysis of feedback trends over time with seasonality adjustments
- **Correlation Analysis:** Relationship analysis between feedback metrics and operational KPIs
- **Root Cause Analysis:** Identification of underlying causes for negative feedback patterns
- **Text Analytics:** Advanced natural language processing for feedback content analysis
- **Customer Segmentation:** Feedback analysis by customer segments and value tiers
- **Competitive Benchmarking:** Industry comparison and competitive positioning analysis

### **4.4 Report Generation**

- **Automated Reports:** Scheduled report generation and distribution to stakeholders
- **Custom Reports:** Flexible report builder with drag-and-drop interface
- **Real-time Alerts:** Immediate notifications for critical feedback and sentiment changes
- **Export Capabilities:** Multiple format exports (PDF, Excel, CSV) for further analysis
- **Interactive Reports:** Drill-down capabilities for detailed analysis
- **Mobile Reports:** Optimized mobile interface for on-the-go access to feedback insights
