Analyze how long inventory has been sitting in stock. crucial for identifying obsolete stock and managing cash flow.

## 2. üë• USERS
*   **Finance Manager**: Provision for depreciation.
*   **Inventory Manager**: Clear out old stock (Discounts/Write-offs).

## 3. üõ†Ô∏è REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Functional
*   **Age Calculation**: Based on Last Receipt Date (FIFO assumption) or Batch Date.
*   **Buckets**: Group into 0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days.
*   **Valuation**: Show value of aged stock.

### 3.2 Data Fields
*   **Item**: SKU, Name.
*   **Last In**: Date of last receipt.
*   **Age (Days)**: `Today - Last In`.
*   **Aging Bucket**: Derived.
*   **Value**: `Qty * Cost`.

### 3.3 Actions
*   **Write-off**: Shortcut to Adjustment (Reason: Obsolete).
*   **Discount**: Shortcut to Price List update.
