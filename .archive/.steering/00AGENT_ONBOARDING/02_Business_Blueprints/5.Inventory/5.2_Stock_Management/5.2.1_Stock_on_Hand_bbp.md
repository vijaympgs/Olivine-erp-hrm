# 5.2.1 STOCK ON HAND

**Type**: OPERATIONAL VIEW  
**Status**: DRAFTING  
**Owner**: Astra

## 1. üéØ OBJECTIVE
Provide a real-time view of current stock levels across the entire organization. This is the primary inquiry screen for inventory visibility.

## 2. üë• USERS
*   **Store Manager**: Check availability for customers.
*   **Warehouse Manager**: Monitor stock levels.
*   **Procurement**: Check current stock before ordering.

## 3. üõ†Ô∏è REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Functional
*   **View Stock**: Display `Inventory` records.
*   **Calculate Available**: Show `On Hand`, `Allocated`, and `Available` quantities.
*   **Filtering**: Filter by Company (Multi-tenant), Location, Category, Brand, SKU.
*   **Search**: Text search by Item Name or SKU.

### 3.2 Data Fields
*   **Item Details**: SKU, Name, UOM, Category.
*   **Quantities**: 
    *   `On Hand` (Physical in warehouse)
    *   `Allocated` (Reserved for Sales Orders)
    *   `Available` (On Hand - Allocated)
*   **Valuation**: Average Cost (optional/permission-based).

### 3.3 Actions
*   **Drill-down**: Click row to see Item Detail or Movement History.
*   **Format**: Grid view with sorting.

## 4. üìä DATA MODEL
*   **Primary Model**: `Inventory` (Read-Only)
*   **References**: `ItemMaster`, `Location`, `Company`
