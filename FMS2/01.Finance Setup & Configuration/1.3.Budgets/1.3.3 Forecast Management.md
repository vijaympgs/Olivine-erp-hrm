## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Forecast Management module provides comprehensive financial forecasting capabilities, enabling data-driven prediction of future financial performance through advanced analytics, trend analysis, and scenario modeling to support strategic planning, budget preparation, and risk management across the organization.

### **2.2 Business Scope**
**In Scope:**
- Revenue forecasting and trend analysis
- Expense prediction and cost modeling
- Cash flow forecasting and liquidity planning
- Budget vs actual variance analysis
- Scenario-based forecasting and what-if analysis
- Forecast accuracy tracking and improvement
- Multi-dimensional forecasting (department, cost center, project)
- Historical data analysis and pattern recognition

**Out of Scope:**
- Actual budget template creation (handled by Budget Templates module)
- Budget approval workflows (handled by Budget Approval Workflow module)
- Financial transaction processing (handled by transaction modules)
- Actual financial reporting (handled by Analytics module)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Finance Executives:** Strategic forecasting and financial planning oversight
- **Budget Managers:** Forecast accuracy and variance analysis
- **Department Heads:** Departmental forecasting and resource planning
- **Financial Analysts:** Forecast modeling and data analysis
- **Strategic Planners:** Long-term financial forecasting and scenario planning

## **4. FUNCTIONAL CAPTURE**

### **4.1 Core Forecasting Capabilities**
- **Revenue Forecasting:** Predict future revenue streams based on historical trends, market conditions, and business drivers
- **Expense Forecasting:** Project future expenses including fixed costs, variable costs, and capital expenditures
- **Cash Flow Forecasting:** Forecast cash inflows and outflows for liquidity planning and working capital management
- **Budget vs Actual Analysis:** Compare forecasted figures with actual results to identify variances and improve accuracy
- **Scenario Modeling:** Create multiple forecast scenarios (optimistic, pessimistic, realistic) for risk assessment and contingency planning

### **4.2 Advanced Analytics Features**
- **Trend Analysis:** Identify and analyze historical patterns, seasonal variations, and growth trends
- **Variance Analysis:** Calculate and visualize forecast accuracy, identify significant deviations, and root cause analysis
- **Predictive Modeling:** Use statistical models and machine learning algorithms for improved forecast accuracy
- **What-If Analysis:** Simulate the impact of different business decisions and market conditions on financial outcomes
- **Rolling Forecasts:** Continuously update forecasts based on new data and changing business conditions

### **4.3 Multi-Dimensional Forecasting**
- **Department-Level Forecasting:** Generate forecasts for individual departments with specific business drivers and assumptions
- **Cost Center Forecasting:** Create detailed forecasts for cost centers with expense categorization and allocation rules
- **Project-Based Forecasting:** Forecast project-specific revenues, costs, and cash flows with timeline-based projections
- **Product/Service Line Forecasting:** Forecast performance by product lines, service categories, or business units
- **Geographic Forecasting:** Generate forecasts by regions, territories, or market segments

### **4.4 Forecast Management Tools**
- **Forecast Configuration:** Set up forecast parameters, time periods, and calculation methods
- **Assumption Management:** Document and manage forecast assumptions, business drivers, and external factors
- **Version Control:** Maintain forecast versions with change tracking and approval workflows
- **Collaboration Tools:** Enable team-based forecasting with role-based access and contribution tracking
- **Export Capabilities:** Export forecasts in various formats (Excel, PDF, CSV) for reporting and sharing
