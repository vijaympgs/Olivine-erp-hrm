## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Tax Compliance module provides comprehensive reporting and management of tax compliance requirements, monitoring systems, and regulatory frameworks, ensuring automated compliance tracking, deadline management, and audit readiness across all tax jurisdictions and regulatory obligations.

### **2.2 Business Scope**
**In Scope:**
- Tax compliance requirement configuration and management
- Regulatory deadline tracking and monitoring
- Compliance check rules and validation frameworks
- Tax audit trail and documentation management
- Multi-jurisdiction compliance monitoring
- Compliance reporting and dashboard analytics
- Risk assessment and compliance scoring
- Automated compliance alerts and notifications

**Out of Scope:**
- Actual tax return filing (handled by Tax Returns module)
- Tax payment processing (handled by Payment modules)
- Financial transaction processing (handled by transaction modules)
- Tax calculation and rate management (handled by Tax Codes & Rates module)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Compliance Officers:** Tax compliance configuration and monitoring
- **Tax Managers:** Compliance requirement management and oversight
- **Finance Controllers:** Compliance risk assessment and reporting
- **Internal Auditors:** Compliance audit trail and documentation
- **External Regulators:** Tax compliance validation and reporting

## **4. FUNCTIONAL CAPTURE**

### **4.1 Compliance Requirements Management**
- **Requirement Configuration:** Define and manage tax compliance requirements for different jurisdictions
- **Regulatory Frameworks:** Support for multiple regulatory frameworks and tax authorities
- **Compliance Types:** Filing, payment, documentation, reporting, registration, and assessment requirements
- **Frequency Management:** Monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annual, ad-hoc, and event-based compliance schedules
- **Deadline Tracking:** Comprehensive due date management with grace periods and penalty calculations

### **4.2 Compliance Monitoring & Tracking**
- **Real-time Monitoring:** Continuous monitoring of compliance status across all requirements
- **Compliance Scoring:** Automated compliance scoring (0-100) with risk level assessment
- **Status Management:** Track compliance status from pending to compliant/non-compliant
- **Assignment System:** Assign compliance tasks to responsible users with escalation workflows
- **Audit Trail:** Complete audit trail of all compliance activities and changes

### **4.3 Compliance Checks & Validation**
- **Automated Checks:** Data validation, calculation checks, documentation verification
- **Deadline Monitoring:** Automated deadline tracking with advance notifications
- **Threshold Monitoring:** Financial threshold checks and limit validations
- **Reconciliation Checks:** Automated reconciliation of tax data across systems
- **Severity Levels:** Low, medium, high, and critical severity classification for compliance issues

### **4.4 Risk Assessment & Analytics**
- **Risk Scoring:** Automated risk assessment based on compliance history and current status
- **Trend Analysis:** Historical compliance trend analysis and predictive risk assessment
- **Heat Maps:** Visual risk heat maps for different jurisdictions and tax types
- **Compliance Metrics:** Key performance indicators for compliance management
- **Exception Reporting:** Detailed reporting of compliance exceptions and remediation actions

### **4.5 Reporting & Dashboard**
- **Compliance Dashboard:** Real-time overview of compliance status across all requirements
- **Regulatory Reports:** Generate compliance reports for internal and external stakeholders
- **Management Reports:** Executive summaries and detailed compliance analytics
- **Audit Reports:** Comprehensive audit trail documentation for compliance verification
- **Performance Reports:** Compliance team performance and productivity metrics

### **4.6 Data Points & Metrics**
- **Compliance Metrics:** Overall compliance rate, requirement-specific compliance scores
- **Deadline Metrics:** On-time compliance rate, average completion time, overdue items
- **Risk Metrics:** Risk distribution, high-risk items, risk trend analysis
- **Operational Metrics:** Check execution time, user productivity, system performance
- **Financial Metrics:** Penalty amounts, compliance costs, cost of non-compliance

### **4.7 Alert & Notification System**
- **Automated Alerts:** Real-time alerts for compliance deadlines and status changes
- **Escalation Workflows:** Multi-level escalation for critical compliance issues
- **Email Notifications:** Automated email notifications for assigned users and management
- **System Notifications:** In-app notifications for immediate compliance updates
- **Custom Alert Rules:** Configurable alert rules based on business requirements

### **4.8 Export & Integration**
- **Export Formats:** PDF, Excel, CSV for compliance reports and audit documentation
- **API Integration:** REST APIs for integration with external compliance systems
- **Data Exchange:** Secure data exchange with tax authorities and regulatory bodies
- **Backup & Archive:** Secure backup and archival of compliance data
- **Retention Management:** Configurable data retention policies for compliance records
