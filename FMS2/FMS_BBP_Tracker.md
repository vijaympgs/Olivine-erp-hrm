## **Complexity Guidelines**

### **Master Modules (_M_):**
- **MST-S:** Simple configuration tables, 2-3 models, basic validation
- **MST-M:** Standard configuration, 4-6 models, moderate business rules
- **MST-C:** Complex configuration, 7+ models, advanced business rules, integrations

### **Transaction Modules (_T_):**
- **TXN-S:** Simple transactions, 2-3 models, basic workflow
- **TXN-M:** Standard transactions, 4-6 models, moderate workflow, approvals
- **TXN-C:** Complex transactions, 7+ models, advanced workflow, integrations, reporting
