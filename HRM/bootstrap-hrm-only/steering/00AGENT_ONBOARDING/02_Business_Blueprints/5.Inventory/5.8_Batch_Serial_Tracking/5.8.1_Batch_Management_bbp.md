Track inventory by manufacturing batches/lots for traceability, expiry management, and quality control.

## 2. üë• USERS
*   **Warehouse Receiver**: Assign batch numbers on receipt.
*   **Quality Manager**: Trace defective batches.

## 3. üõ†Ô∏è REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Functional
*   **Batch Creation**: Auto-generate or manual entry during Goods Receipt.
*   **Expiry Tracking**: Associate Expiry Date with Batch.
*   **Stock by Batch**: Inventory records are split by Batch Number.

### 3.2 Data Fields (Model: `Batch`)
*   `batch_number` (String): Unique ID.
*   `item`: FK to ItemMaster.
*   `manufacturing_date`: Date.
*   `expiry_date`: Date.
*   `supplier_batch_ref`: Vendor's batch #.
*   `status`: Active, Restricted, Expired.

### 3.3 Integration
*   **Transactions**: Stock Movements must reference `batch_id` for batch-tracked items.
