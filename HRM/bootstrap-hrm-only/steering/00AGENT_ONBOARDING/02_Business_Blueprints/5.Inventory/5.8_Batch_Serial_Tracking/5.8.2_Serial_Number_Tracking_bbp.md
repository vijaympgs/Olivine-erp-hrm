## 3. üõ†Ô∏è REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Functional
*   **Uniqueness**: Serial Number must be unique per Item (or globally).
*   **1-to-1 Mapping**: Process qty of 1 per serial number line.
*   **Lifecycle**: Pending -> In Stock -> Sold -> Returned.

### 3.2 Data Fields (Model: `SerialNumber`)
*   `serial_number` (String).
*   `item`: FK Component.
*   `current_location`: FK Location.
*   `status`: Available, Allocated, Shipped, Defective.

### 3.3 Validation
*   Cannot have duplicate serials for same item.
*   Outbound transaction must specify exact serial numbers being moved.
