### 3.1 Functional
*   **Policy Definition**: Define rules per Item+Location combination.
*   **Replenishment Methods**:
    *   **Fixed Reorder Point**: Order when stock < X.
    *   **Min-Max**: Order to bring stock up to Max when it hits Min.
    *   *(Future)* Demand Driven.
*   **Lead Time**: Store vendor lead time for calculation.

### 3.2 Data Fields (Model: `ReorderPolicy`)
*   `item`: FK to ItemMaster.
*   `location`: FK to Location.
*   `reorder_point` (qty): Trigger level.
*   `reorder_quantity` (qty): Fixed amount to order (optional).
*   `min_quantity` (qty): For Min-Max.
*   `max_quantity` (qty): For Min-Max.
*   `safety_stock` (qty): Buffer.
*   `preferred_vendor`: FK to Vendor.

### 3.3 Validation
*   Reorder Point must be >= Safety Stock.
*   Max Quantity must be > Min Quantity.
