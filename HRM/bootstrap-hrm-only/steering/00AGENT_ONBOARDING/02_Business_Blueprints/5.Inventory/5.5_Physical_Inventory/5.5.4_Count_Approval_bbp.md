# 5.5.4 Count Approval
Template Ref: _rule_01 (Governance Rule)

## 5.5.4.1 Business Purpose

Governs the authorization of **Stock Take Variances**. Ensures that large discrepancies between system stock and physical count are investigated by a supervisor before the ledger is adjusted.

## 5.5.4.2 Logic & Rules

### A) Approval Triggers
- **Value Threshold**: Total variance value > `$X`.
- **Quantity Threshold**: Single item variance % > `10%`.
- **Random Audit**: 5% of all stock takes require approval for quality control.
