## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Sales Reports module serves as the comprehensive data reporting and analytics system for generating, managing, and distributing sales performance insights across the organization. It provides users with powerful tools for creating custom sales reports, analyzing revenue trends, tracking team performance, and monitoring key sales metrics. The module enables comprehensive sales visibility with real-time data aggregation, interactive visualizations, customizable report formats, and automated distribution to ensure data-driven decision-making, improved sales strategy, and enhanced performance management through detailed sales analytics and actionable business insights.

### **2.2 Business Scope**

**In Scope:**
- Comprehensive sales performance reporting with revenue, pipeline, and conversion analytics
- Custom report builder with drag-and-drop interface and flexible data source configuration
- Real-time sales dashboards with live data updates and interactive visualizations
- Team and individual performance tracking with goal achievement and productivity metrics
- Sales pipeline analysis with stage conversion rates and forecasting accuracy
- Customer segmentation reporting with behavioral analysis and lifetime value calculations
- Product and service sales analysis with performance trends and profitability metrics
- Automated report scheduling with customizable delivery formats and distribution lists
- Mobile-responsive interface for on-the-go sales data access and quick insights
- Export capabilities with multiple format options (PDF, Excel, CSV) and customizable templates

**Out of Scope:**
- Real-time sales transaction processing and order management (handled by sales modules)
- Advanced customer relationship management and contact data management (handled by contact modules)
- Sales workflow automation and process management (handled by sales process modules)
- Advanced financial accounting and billing systems (handled by external finance systems)
- Marketing campaign execution and lead generation (handled by marketing modules)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Sales Representatives:** Access personal performance reports and track individual sales metrics
- **Sales Managers:** Monitor team performance, analyze pipeline health, and manage sales forecasts
- **Sales Directors:** Review regional performance, analyze market trends, and strategic planning
- **Executives:** Access comprehensive sales analytics, revenue insights, and business intelligence
- **Financial Analysts:** Analyze sales data for financial planning and revenue forecasting
- **Marketing Teams:** Review sales attribution data and campaign effectiveness metrics

## **4. REPORTING & ANALYTICS**

### **4.1 Key Performance Indicators**

- **Revenue Metrics:** Total sales revenue, revenue growth rate, average deal size, and revenue per sales rep
- **Pipeline Analytics:** Pipeline value, conversion rates by stage, sales cycle length, and forecast accuracy
- **Team Performance:** Individual and team quota achievement, activity metrics, and productivity ratios
- **Customer Analytics:** Customer acquisition cost, customer lifetime value, retention rates, and churn analysis
- **Product Performance:** Product sales volume, profitability analysis, and cross-sell/up-sell metrics
- **Geographic Analysis:** Regional sales performance, market penetration, and territory effectiveness
- **Time-based Analytics:** Monthly/quarterly/annual trends, seasonality analysis, and year-over-year comparisons
- **Forecast Accuracy:** Sales forecast vs. actual performance, variance analysis, and prediction confidence

### **4.2 Standard Report Templates**

#### **Executive Sales Dashboard**
- Revenue overview with current period vs. previous period comparisons
- Sales team performance ranking with achievement percentages
- Pipeline health indicators with stage distribution and conversion rates
- Top performing products/services with revenue contribution analysis
- Geographic performance heatmap with regional market insights
- Key risk indicators with alert thresholds and trend analysis

#### **Team Performance Report**
- Individual sales representative performance with quota achievement
- Activity metrics breakdown (calls, emails, meetings, demos)
- Pipeline analysis with deal stage distribution and velocity
- Customer acquisition and retention metrics by team member
- Performance trends with month-over-month comparisons
- Coaching opportunities with skill gap analysis

#### **Sales Pipeline Analysis**
- Current pipeline value with stage-wise distribution
- Conversion rate analysis by pipeline stage and time period
- Deal velocity metrics with average time in each stage
- Pipeline health indicators with stale deal identification
- Forecast accuracy with confidence intervals and risk factors
- Win/loss analysis with reason categorization and trends

#### **Revenue Recognition Report**
- Revenue recognition by period with accrual and cash basis comparisons
- Product/service revenue breakdown with profitability analysis
- Customer segment revenue contribution with growth trends
- Geographic revenue distribution with market share analysis
- Recurring vs. non-recurring revenue with retention metrics
- Revenue forecasting with predictive models and confidence intervals

### **4.3 Advanced Analytics Features**

- **Predictive Analytics:** Machine learning models for sales forecasting and trend prediction
- **What-if Analysis:** Scenario modeling for target setting and resource planning
- **Cohort Analysis:** Customer cohort performance with retention and lifetime value
- **Funnel Analysis:** Detailed conversion funnel with drop-off point identification
- **Correlation Analysis:** Relationship analysis between activities and sales outcomes
- **Benchmarking:** Performance comparison against industry standards and historical data

### **4.4 Export and Distribution Capabilities**

- **Automated Scheduling:** Configurable report generation with cron-based scheduling
- **Multi-format Export:** PDF, Excel, CSV, and PowerPoint export with customizable templates
- **Email Distribution:** Automated email delivery with personalized content and attachments
- **API Integration:** RESTful API for programmatic report access and integration
- **Dashboard Embedding:** Secure report embedding in external portals and applications
- **Mobile Optimization:** Responsive design for mobile access with touch-optimized interactions
- **Real-time Updates:** Live data refresh with configurable update intervals
- **Collaboration Features:** Report sharing with comments, annotations, and discussion threads
