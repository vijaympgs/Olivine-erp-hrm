## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Revenue Tracking Report provides comprehensive analysis and monitoring of revenue generation across the sales organization. It enables sales teams and management to track revenue performance against targets, analyze revenue trends by various dimensions, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions for revenue optimization and strategic planning.

### **2.2 Business Scope**
**In Scope:**
- Revenue tracking and analysis by time period, product, and customer segment
- Sales performance against quotas and targets with variance analysis
- Revenue trend analysis and growth rate calculations
- Product and service revenue performance comparison and profitability analysis
- Customer segment revenue analysis and lifetime value tracking
- Geographic and regional revenue distribution and market penetration analysis
- Automated revenue reporting and forecasting capabilities
- Historical revenue data analysis and predictive revenue insights

**Out of Revenue Tracking:**
- Real-time transaction processing and order management
- Customer relationship management and follow-up activities
- Quote generation and proposal management
- Inventory management and product configuration
- Advanced financial planning and budgeting functions
- Complex revenue recognition and accounting compliance

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Sales Representatives:** Monitor personal revenue performance against targets and quotas
- **Sales Managers:** Analyze team revenue performance, coach representatives, set revenue targets
- **Sales Directors:** Review regional revenue performance, strategic planning, market analysis
- **Sales Leadership:** Executive-level revenue analytics, strategic decision making, performance benchmarking
- **Finance Teams:** Revenue forecasting, financial planning, budget analysis, compliance reporting
- **Marketing Teams:** Campaign revenue attribution, ROI analysis, marketing budget optimization
- **Executive Management:** Corporate revenue performance, strategic planning, investor relations

## **4. FUNCTIONAL CAPTURE**

### **4.1 Core Revenue Metrics**
- **Overall Revenue Performance:**
  - Total Revenue (Month-to-Date, Quarter-to-Date, Year-to-Date)
  - Revenue Growth Rate (period-over-period, year-over-year)
  - Revenue vs. Target/Quota Achievement (percentage and variance)
  - Average Deal Size and Revenue per Transaction
  - Revenue per Sales Representative and Team Performance

- **Time-Based Revenue Analysis:**
  - Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Annual Revenue Trends
  - Seasonal Revenue Patterns and Cyclical Analysis
  - Revenue Velocity and Sales Cycle Impact Analysis
  - Revenue Forecast Accuracy and Variance Analysis
  - Historical Revenue Performance and Trend Projections

- **Revenue Quality Metrics:**
  - Recurring Revenue vs. One-Time Revenue Analysis
  - High-Margin vs. Low-Margin Revenue Performance
  - Revenue Concentration and Customer Dependency Analysis
  - Revenue Stability and Predictability Metrics
  - Revenue Recognition Compliance and Accounting Standards

### **4.2 Product and Service Revenue Analysis**

## **5. UI/UX SPECIFICATIONS**

  - Intuitive report browsing with clear section organization

  - Breadcrumb navigation for drill-down data exploration

  - Mobile-optimized report viewing with touch-friendly controls

### **5.2 User Experience Design**
