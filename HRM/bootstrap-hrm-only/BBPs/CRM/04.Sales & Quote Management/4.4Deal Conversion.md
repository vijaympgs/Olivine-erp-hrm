## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
The Deal Conversion Dashboard serves as the comprehensive analytics and visualization system for tracking, monitoring, and optimizing the conversion of quotes and opportunities into closed deals. It provides sales teams, managers, and executives with real-time insights into conversion rates, sales performance, deal velocity, and revenue forecasting. The dashboard enables data-driven decision making through interactive visualizations, trend analysis, and performance benchmarking to improve sales effectiveness and revenue outcomes.

### **2.2 Business Scope**

**In Scope:**
- Real-time deal conversion tracking with visual dashboards and interactive charts
- Quote-to-deal conversion rate analysis with trend monitoring and performance benchmarking
- Sales pipeline velocity analysis with time-based metrics and bottleneck identification
- Revenue forecasting with predictive analytics and confidence scoring
- Team and individual performance tracking with leaderboards and achievement metrics
- Deal stage analysis with conversion funnel visualization and drop-off analysis
- Product and service performance analysis with top-selling items and margin analysis
- Customer segment analysis with conversion patterns and profitability insights
- Mobile-responsive dashboard design for on-the-go sales performance monitoring
- Export and reporting capabilities with scheduled delivery and custom formatting

**Out of Scope:**
- Quote creation and management (handled by Quote Creation module)
- Opportunity management and pipeline updates (handled by Opportunity Pipeline module)
- Price management and discount approval (handled by Price Management module)
- Customer service and support case management (handled by Customer Service modules)
- Advanced financial accounting and billing processes (handled by external ERP systems)

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Sales Representatives:** Monitor personal conversion performance, track deal progress, and identify improvement opportunities
- **Sales Managers:** Analyze team performance, coach representatives, and optimize sales strategies
- **Sales Executives:** Review overall sales performance, revenue forecasting, and strategic planning
- **Sales Operations:** Monitor system performance, data quality, and process optimization
- **Finance Teams:** Track revenue recognition, forecasting accuracy, and financial planning
- **Marketing Teams:** Analyze lead quality and campaign effectiveness on conversion rates

```python
class DealConversionMetric(models.Model):
    """
    Primary model representing deal conversion metrics and analytics data
    """
    
    # Primary Key
    id = models.UUIDField(primary_key=True, default=uuid.uuid4, editable=False)
    
    # Foreign Keys
    company_id = models.UUIDField()
    user_id = models.UUIDField(null=True, blank=True)  # For individual metrics
    team_id = models.UUIDField(null=True, blank=True)   # For team metrics
    
    # Metric Information
    metric_type = models.CharField(max_length=50, choices=METRIC_TYPE_CHOICES)
    metric_period = models.CharField(max_length=20, choices=PERIOD_CHOICES)
    period_start_date = models.DateField()
    period_end_date = models.DateField()
    
    # Conversion Metrics
    quotes_created = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    quotes_sent = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    quotes_accepted = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    quotes_rejected = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    conversion_rate = models.DecimalField(max_digits=5, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    
    # Revenue Metrics
    total_quote_value = models.DecimalField(max_digits=15, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    converted_value = models.DecimalField(max_digits=15, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    average_deal_size = models.DecimalField(max_digits=15, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    
    # Time Metrics
    average_conversion_time = models.IntegerField(default=0)  # in days
    average_quote_response_time = models.IntegerField(default=0)  # in hours
    
    # Metadata
    created_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now_add=True)
    updated_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now=True)
    
    class Meta:
        db_table = 'crm_deal_conversion_metrics'
        verbose_name = 'Deal Conversion Metric'
        verbose_name_plural = 'Deal Conversion Metrics'
        indexes = [
            models.Index(fields=['company_id', 'metric_period', 'period_start_date'], name='idx_company_period'),
            models.Index(fields=['user_id'], name='idx_user'),
            models.Index(fields=['team_id'], name='idx_team'),
            models.Index(fields=['metric_type'], name='idx_metric_type'),
        ]
```

```python
class ConversionFunnel(models.Model):

    period_end_date = models.DateField()
    
    # Funnel Stages
    leads_count = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    opportunities_count = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    quotes_count = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    accepted_quotes_count = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    closed_deals_count = models.IntegerField(default=0)
    
    # Conversion Rates
    lead_to_opportunity_rate = models.DecimalField(max_digits=5, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    opportunity_to_quote_rate = models.DecimalField(max_digits=5, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    quote_to_acceptance_rate = models.DecimalField(max_digits=5, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    acceptance_to_close_rate = models.DecimalField(max_digits=5, decimal_places=2, default=0)
    
    # Metadata
    created_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now_add=True)
    updated_at = models.DateTimeField(auto_now=True)
    
    class Meta:
        db_table = 'crm_conversion_funnels'
        verbose_name = 'Conversion Funnel'
        verbose_name_plural = 'Conversion Funnels'
        indexes = [
            models.Index(fields=['company_id', 'funnel_period', 'period_start_date'], name='idx_company_funnel'),
        ]
```
