# 03.4 Offer Management

## 03.4.1 Business Purpose

Offer Management serves as the critical bridge between candidate selection and employee onboarding, providing a structured workflow for creating, managing, and tracking job offers throughout the recruitment process. It enables HR professionals to generate standardized offer letters, manage offer approvals, track candidate responses, and ensure compliance with compensation policies while maintaining audit trails for all offer-related activities. The system streamlines the offer-to-hire conversion process and provides analytics for offer effectiveness and recruitment metrics.

## 03.4.2 Business Scope

Provide comprehensive offer management workflow with automated offer letter generation, multi-level approval processes, candidate communication tracking, and integration with compensation planning. Support verbal offer management, offer revision workflows, electronic signature capabilities, and real-time status tracking. The system ensures compliance with compensation guidelines, maintains offer history, provides analytics for offer acceptance rates, and integrates with onboarding processes for seamless new hire transitions.

## 03.4.6 Business Rules & Validations

### **Data Integrity Rules**
- Offer numbers must be unique within company
- Offer sent date cannot be before candidate interview date
- Response deadline must be after offer sent date
- Salary must comply with compensation bands for position grade
- Approval workflow must be completed before sending offer

### **Validation Logic**
```python
def clean_proposed_salary(self):
    """
    Validate proposed salary against compensation bands
    """
    if self.proposed_salary and self.salary_grade:
        min_salary, max_salary = self.get_salary_range_for_grade(self.salary_grade)
        if self.proposed_salary < min_salary or self.proposed_salary > max_salary:
            raise ValidationError(f"Proposed salary must be within {min_salary} - {max_salary} for grade {self.salary_grade}")

def clean_response_deadline(self):
    """
    Validate response deadline is reasonable
    """
    if self.response_deadline and self.offer_sent_date:
        max_deadline_days = 30  # Company policy
        deadline_diff = (self.response_deadline - self.offer_sent_date).days
        if deadline_diff > max_deadline_days:
            raise ValidationError(f"Response deadline cannot be more than {max_deadline_days} days from offer sent date")
```

### **Business Constraints**
- Offers cannot be sent without required approvals
- Salary revisions require additional approval levels
- Expired offers cannot be reactivated without new approval
- Declined offers require 6-month cooling period before reconsideration

## 03.4.9 API Specifications

### **9.1 REST Endpoints**

#### **GET /api/offers/**
- **Purpose:** Retrieve list of offers with filtering and pagination
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Parameters:** status, candidate_id, position_id, date_from, date_to, page, page_size
- **Response:** Paginated list of offers with full details

#### **POST /api/offers/**
- **Purpose:** Create new offer for candidate
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Request Body:** Complete offer details with candidate and position information
- **Response:** Created offer with ID and initial status

#### **PUT /api/offers/{id}/send/**
- **Purpose:** Send offer to candidate and initiate workflow
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Parameters:** offer ID
- **Request Body:** Email template and delivery options
- **Response:** Updated offer status and delivery confirmation

#### **POST /api/offers/{id}/approvals/**
- **Purpose:** Submit offer for approval
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Parameters:** offer ID
- **Request Body:** Approval level and comments
- **Response:** Approval workflow initiation confirmation

### **9.2 Data Formats**
- **Request Format:** JSON with UTF-8 encoding
- **Response Format:** JSON with consistent field naming (snake_case)
- **Error Format:** JSON with error code, message, and details field
