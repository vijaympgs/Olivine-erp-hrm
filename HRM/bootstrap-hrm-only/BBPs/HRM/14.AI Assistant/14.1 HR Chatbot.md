## **2. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**
To provide intelligent, automated HR support through an AI-powered chatbot that can handle employee inquiries, provide instant responses to HR-related questions, and assist with common HR tasks 24/7, reducing HR team workload and improving employee experience.

### **2.2 Business Scope**
**In Scope:**
- Employee HR policy inquiries and responses
- Benefits and compensation information queries
- Leave balance and request assistance
- Payroll and tax-related questions
- Onboarding and offboarding guidance
- Training and development recommendations
- Performance management support
- HR process automation and workflow assistance

**Out of Scope:**
- Complex HR decision-making requiring human judgment
- Sensitive employee relations issues
- Legal compliance advice
- Disciplinary actions handling
- Strategic HR planning

### **2.3 Key Stakeholders**
- **Employees:** Primary users seeking instant HR support
- **HR Team:** Supervisors managing chatbot knowledge and responses
- **IT Department:** Technical support and integration management
- **Management:** Analytics and insights from chatbot interactions
- **Compliance Team:** Ensuring regulatory compliance in responses

## **6. BUSINESS RULES & VALIDATIONS**

### **6.1 Data Integrity Rules**
- Chatbot API keys must be encrypted at rest
- Knowledge base articles require HR verification before activation
- Escalation assignments must be to active HR personnel
- Satisfaction ratings must be between 1-5
- Confidence scores must be between 0.0-1.0

### **6.2 Validation Logic**
```python
def clean_confidence_threshold(self):
    """
    Validate confidence threshold is within acceptable range
    """
    if self.confidence_threshold < 0.0 or self.confidence_threshold > 1.0:
        raise ValidationError("Confidence threshold must be between 0.0 and 1.0")

def clean_api_key_encrypted(self):
    """
    Validate API key encryption and format
    """
    if not self.api_key_encrypted:
        raise ValidationError("API key is required for chatbot functionality")
```

### **6.3 Business Constraints**
- Only one active chatbot per company
- Knowledge base articles must have unique questions within categories
- Escalated conversations must be assigned within 24 hours
- Chatbot responses cannot provide legal or medical advice
- Personal data must be masked in chatbot responses

## **9. API SPECIFICATIONS**

### **9.1 REST Endpoints**

#### **POST /api/hr/chatbot/conversation/start**
- **Purpose:** Initialize new chatbot conversation
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Request Body:** {"chatbot_id": "uuid", "employee_id": "uuid"}
- **Response:** {"conversation_id": "uuid", "session_id": "string", "welcome_message": "string"}

#### **POST /api/hr/chatbot/message/send**
- **Purpose:** Send message to chatbot and get response
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Request Body:** {"conversation_id": "uuid", "message": "string", "message_type": "user"}
- **Response:** {"message_id": "uuid", "response": "string", "confidence_score": 0.95, "suggested_actions": []}

#### **GET /api/hr/chatbot/knowledge/search**
- **Purpose:** Search knowledge base for quick answers
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Parameters:** q=string, category=string, limit=10
- **Response:** {"articles": [{"id": "uuid", "question": "string", "answer": "string"}]}

#### **POST /api/hr/chatbot/escalate**
- **Purpose:** Escalate conversation to human HR representative
- **Authentication:** JWT token required
- **Request Body:** {"conversation_id": "uuid", "reason": "string", "urgency": "medium"}
- **Response:** {"escalation_id": "uuid", "assigned_hr_user": "string", "estimated_response_time": "2 hours"}

### **9.2 Data Formats**
- **Request Format:** JSON with UTF-8 encoding
- **Response Format:** JSON with standardized error structure
- **Error Format:** {"error": {"code": "string", "message": "string", "details": {}}}
